What is ANGC?

Ancient Golden Coin (ANGC) is a Solana-based cultural digital asset designed as a modern on-chain relic. It emphasizes symbolism, permanence, and transparency rather than financial promises.

Design Philosophy

ANGC has a fixed and final supply. All core mechanics are verifiable on-chain, with liquidity locked to reinforce long-term transparency.

The project follows a community-led and intentionally teamless structure, prioritizing immutability over authority.

ANGC is positioned as a cultural artifact — a digital expression of ancient value interpreted through modern blockchain infrastructure.
